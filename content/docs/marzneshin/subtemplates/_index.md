When you enter the link to your sub in the browser, you will see a simple HTML page displaying user information based on the provided sub link. Thanks to the hard work of the community developers, there are many different templates available to beautify and customize the page. Each of these templates has its own unique design and features. Below is a random selection of templates:

### Available Templates:
